About

Empyrion Codex

Made by Dr. Dark

A community-made tool for browsing scenario data from Empyrion - Galactic Survival. Explore items, blocks, and traders from any scenario — seamlessly, with no installation required. Everything runs entirely in your browser, and none of your scenario data ever leaves your device.

Look up any item, block, or trader and get the most important information at a glance. Load a scenario folder directly from your disk, export and re-import the data as a compact .empcdx file, and browse saved scenarios — all stored privately in your browser's IndexedDB cache.

Inspiration

Empyrion Codex is inspired by EmpyrionBuddy, with the goal of modernising the concept and making it even more intuitive. Unlike EmpyrionBuddy, this tool lets you load any scenario and explore it directly in the Codex. That said, EmpyrionBuddy shines in its own right and is well worth checking out.

Empyrion Codex is also open-source, meaning anyone can view the source code, report issues, or suggest improvements. If you're a developer, visit the GitHub repository below.

Open Source

Contributions, bug reports, and feature requests are all welcome. Feel free to open an issue or submit a pull request on GitHub.

Intellectual Property

Icons and other assets that may appear on this site are the intellectual property of Eleon Game Studios, the respective scenario creator, or both. Their display here is purely for identification purposes within the context of this fan tool.

Your Data Stays on Your Computer

All scenario files and configuration data you load are parsed entirely in your browser. No files, game data, or personal information are ever uploaded to or stored on any server.

This site uses Plausible Analytics to collect basic, anonymous usage statistics — specifically which pages are visited and which featured scenarios are downloaded. Plausible is a privacy-first, open-source analytics tool: it uses no cookies, collects no personal data, and is fully GDPR-compliant. You can opt out at any time under .

This site is served via Cloudflare, which may process standard request metadata (such as IP addresses) as part of its infrastructure. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy for details.
